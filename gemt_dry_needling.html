My Body Feels "Off"

Everyday discomfort is nothing to scoff at. One small ache a day is 365 little interruptions a year. Let us take that load off.

1. Stiffness That Follows You Around

That tight spot in your neck or back doesn’t just vanish. It lingers through your workday, into your commute, and even when you try to rest — weighing you down more than you realize.

2. Small Aches, Big Distractions

It’s not unbearable, but it’s always there. A nagging knot, a dull ache, a shoulder that just won’t loosen. These little distractions quietly eat away at your focus and energy every day.

3. Movement Feels “Off”

You’re not injured, but you’re not moving freely either. A twist feels restricted, your back feels heavy, or your leg feels tight after a short walk. It’s subtle — but it’s not how your body should feel.

4. Relief Never Lasts

Stretching helps… for a while. A massage loosens things… but by the next day, the tension is back. When nothing seems to give lasting relief, it’s a sign the muscles need a deeper reset.

5. You’ve Started Ignoring It

Maybe you tell yourself, “It’s just part of aging,” or “It’s not that bad.” But tension isn’t normal. With GEMT dry needling, we target the root of the problem so you can finally stop brushing it off.

This is what we'll do

Identify - Prioritize - Treat - Enjoy
You'll be surprised how a little nudge to the body can make a direct impact to how you experience your everyday

Step 1: Initial assessment: find the source of your discomfort

We’ll do a focused movement and palpation check, review your surgical history and daily habits, and map the exact muscle spots or scar restrictions that are driving your complaints. No vague labels — we identify specific targets to treat.

Step 2: Choose your tackle points

We’ll build a prioritized list of problem spots — the “tackle points.” You decide which ones to tackle first based on what bothers you most. We’ll explain what each target means and how addressing it will change how you move and feel.

Step 3: Start therapy & track everything

We apply dry needling to the identified targets and monitor your immediate and short-term responses. You’ll chart any differences — pain, stiffness, sleep, movement — even small changes matter. We adjust technique and focus based on what you report.

Step 4: Continue, one by one, until the list is cleared

We work through the tackle points in order, integrating rehab exercises and mobility work as needed, until the selected issues are resolved or significantly improved. Then we set a simple maintenance plan to keep the gains.
